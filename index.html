QUALITIES

Calmness

When it comes to handle pressure at the peak times, MS has shown unmatched calmness which he used to revert the destiny of matches. The calmness that he displays in every match has proven to be his most valueable asset to claim victory over his opponents.

Foresighted

MS has got a deep understanding of players playing patterns and uses his prediction ability to place fielders where the player might play. He has been able to pick up quick wickets by guiding bowlers and fielders through this ability.

His composure, his game reading ability and how he assesses the situation and respects that is what makes him the best in the world.

~Kumar Sangakkara

Leadership

MS is indeed famous for his leadership quality which he has handled with great compassion. He led the Indian Team for almost a decade and succeeded in winning trophies in all formats. His most noted achievements are the 2007 and 2011 World Cup.

I think he's one of the game's greatest-ever leaders. What he's done for the Indian cricket team, and all the various teams he's played for, has been remarkable.

~Ricky Ponting

Known unknown Facts about MS

MS Dhoni used to work for the Indian Railways ▾

Dhoni had an unexpected career before he ventured into the cricket world. From 2001 to 2003, he worked as a Train Ticket Examiner (TTE) at the Kharagpur Railway Station under South Eastern Railway in Midnapore.

He was discovered in a small talent spotting event in 2003 ▾

The player got his big break at a cricket match in Jamshedpur in 2003, as part of BCCI's small-town talent-spotting initiative.
